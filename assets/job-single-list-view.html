We need a web designer Full time

Banana inc.

Art/design Cupertino CA, USA $60/hour

Company description

Our startup was founded in 2014 managing the website migrations of a family of web hosting companies in Belgium. Since then we have gradually expanded our expertise. Currently Zertios is working as a micro-scale service centre for tens of thousands of customers with more than 100 000 websites, providing solutions for system monitoring, support, web development, digital marketing and looking to add more…

Job description

As a Web Developer/designer you will be involved in the design of different visuals for our customer’s corporate website and its brands. You will be working in the web development team managing a portfolio of different hosting brands with multiple transactions every month. You will help improve and maintain the online image and marketability of the company and its products with various smart design solutions – while keeping in line with its current vision.
You will work independently as a distant team member, but you will be strongly engaged in internal projects (various websites, promos, campaign pages, ...) You will be in from the briefing until the final result.

required skills

Design skills

  • You have a webdesign background and can prove it with a beautiful portfolio
  • You are mainly interested to further develop your career in front-end development.
  • Excellent command of Adobe products

Language skills

  • Excellent verbal skills in English are a must

Personal skills

  • Reliable and loyal
  • Responsible
  • Flexible
  • Independent
  • Self-improvement

what you will do

  • Your first task will be to get familiar with look and feel of the brands you will manage.
  • Designing and maintaining projects on our customer’s corporate website and its various brands
  • Create designs of pages with the look and feel of the Brand
  • Code the designs online
  • Banners and images for commercial campaigns

what to expect

  • On our 1st interview we will talk about your experience. We will assess your skills in English. We will discuss your portfolio.
  • On our 2nd interview we give you a challenging task to experience your skill.
  • On our 3rd interview you will get to meet the client you will be working for.

WHAT WE DO FOR YOU

  • Paid training
  • Cool office location in the very center of Sofia
  • Free energy boost – tea, coffee, fresh fruit
  • Competitive salary
  • Social benefits
  • Career future
